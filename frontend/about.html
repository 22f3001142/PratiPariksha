About PratiPariksha

PratiPariksha is a modern CBT platform designed to transform examinations into intelligent, fair, and student-centric learning experiences.

What It Combines

Computer-based tests, teacher analytics, question-bank management, student forums, study resources, and a guided AI revision assistant.

Why It Matters

The aim is to make assessment feel less opaque by turning each attempt into actionable feedback for both the learner and the teacher.
